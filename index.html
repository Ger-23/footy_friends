About Us

Missing out on your favourite cardio kick because you're having trouble getting everyone together for a game? Whether it's work or family obligations or frequent injuries preventing your team getting together, or you just want to meet new and fun people, we can help! Leave the organising to us so that you can concentrate on the playing!
Here's how it works: Once you've signed up, we'll contact you each match day to confirm your availability. Then we'll give you all the information you need to join and play. It's that simple!
